Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are the ideal breakfast food. It's nigh inarguable. When paired with eggs and bacon, pancakes become essentially unstoppable in the arena of breakfast food combat.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  3. .5 tablespoon salt
  4. 1 tablespoon sugar
  5. 2 eggs
  6. 1.5 cups milk
  7. 2 tablespoons melted butter

Process

  • Preheat your skillet.
  • Mix wet ingredients in one bowl, dry ingredients in another. Mix each bowl.
  • Mix contents of both bowls loosely.
  • Drop ladlefulls of batter in hot pan and cook each side evenly.
  • Enjoy!
