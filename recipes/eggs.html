Eggs

Description

Whether you enjoy them scrambled, over easy, or (god forbid) hard boiled, eggs are the food of the common man. Cheap, protein-packed, delicious, and easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Eggs
  • ~1 Tbsp Butter
  • Pinch Of Salt
  • Pinch Of Pepper

Process

  1. Melt the butter in a hot skillet
  2. Once butter has melted, crack the eggs into the pan
  3. Cook until yolks and whites are at desired consistency
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
  5. Enjoy with bacon
